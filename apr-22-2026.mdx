# HockeyStack Revenue Agents: Now Live!

Your CRM, call recordings, and engagement data contain the complete blueprint of how your company wins deals. HockeyStack Revenue Agents extracts it and puts it to work.

**Two agents. One system.**

**Prospecting Agent** creates pipeline — breaks into new target accounts and generates expansion opportunities within your existing customer base. Identifies intent signals, finds the right champion, and builds personalized outreach strategies.

**Deal Agent** closes pipeline and protects revenue — moves open deals forward, manages renewals, and monitors customer health. Assesses every deal against your winning patterns, identifies blockers, and tells reps exactly what to do next.

**How it works**

HockeyStack's ML pipeline analyzes every won and lost deal in your history to discover the specific patterns that drive outcomes — which actions, stakeholders, timing, and engagement sequences separate wins from losses. Agents then execute on those patterns across every rep, every deal, automatically.

This isn't generic AI suggestions. Every action is grounded in what your data says actually works.

**What your team gets**

- Reps get a prioritized daily queue with next actions, drafted comms, account research, and meeting prep — ready to go every morning.
- Managers see which deals are drifting from the winning pattern and where coaching will have the highest leverage — without hours of pipeline reviews.
- Scores, signals, and insights write back to Salesforce, embed in your page layouts, and route to Slack.

**Self-improving by design**

Every completed task, dismissed recommendation, and closed deal feeds back into the blueprint. The system gets sharper the longer it runs — no manual tuning needed.
