## Overview

Account Segments define your ideal customer profile (ICP) criteria. Each segment describes a type of company you want to target — the requirements it must meet and the characteristics that make it an even better fit. Segments are used in prospecting motions to control which accounts receive outreach.

## Where segments are used

| Feature | How segments apply |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Prospecting motions** | Each motion targets one or more account segments, focusing outreach on companies that match |
| **Account scoring** | Segment criteria inform how accounts are evaluated and prioritized |

## How to create a segment

1. Navigate to **Settings** and open the **Account Segments** section.
2. Click **Add Segment**.
3. Fill in the fields:
   * **Name** (required) — A descriptive label (e.g., "Mid-Market SaaS", "Enterprise FinTech").
   * **Requirements** — Describe the hard criteria a company must meet to qualify for this segment. Be specific: industry, employee count, tech stack, geography, or any other firmographic or behavioral criteria.
   * **Nice to Haves** — Describe characteristics that make a company an even stronger fit but are not required. These help the AI prioritize within the segment.
4. Click **Save**.

## Writing effective segment definitions

The AI reads your segment descriptions when evaluating companies and generating tasks. Specificity matters.

**Good segment definition:**

* **Name:** Mid-Market B2B SaaS
* **Requirements:** B2B SaaS company. 200–2,000 employees. North America or Europe. Uses Salesforce as CRM. Has a dedicated sales team of at least 10 AEs.
* **Nice to Haves:** Recently raised Series B or later. Has Gong or similar call recording tool. Growing headcount in sales roles over the past 6 months.

**Bad segment definition:**

* **Name:** Good Companies
* **Requirements:** Companies that could use our product.
* **Nice to Haves:** Big ones.

## How to edit or delete a segment

* **Edit**: Click the edit icon on a segment row. Modify any field and click **Save**.
* **Delete**: Click the delete icon. A confirmation dialog appears before deletion.

## Constraints

* Segment name is required. Requirements and Nice to Haves are optional but strongly recommended for AI quality.
* Deleting a segment does not affect existing prospecting tasks that were generated while the segment was active.
* Changes to segments take effect on the next prospecting task generation cycle.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title={"How many segments should I create?"}>
  Create one segment per distinct ICP you target. Most teams have 2–4 segments (e.g., Enterprise, Mid-Market, or split by vertical). Avoid creating overlapping segments — if two segments would match the same companies, consolidate them.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title={"Can I use the same segment across multiple motions?"}>
  Yes. A segment can be assigned to any number of prospecting motions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title={"Do segments affect deal task generation?"}>
  No. Segments are used in prospecting task generation only. Deal tasks are generated based on deal context, not segment criteria.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
